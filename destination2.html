Pagsanjan Falls

Pagsanjan Falls, also known as Cavinti Falls or Magdapio Falls, is a popular waterfall located in the province of Laguna, Philippines. It's a major tourist destination, reached by a dugout canoe trip on the river known as "Shooting the Rapids" or by a short hike from Cavinti. The boat ride has been a popular attraction for centuries, with records dating back to 1894. The waterfall is located at the confluence of the Balanac River and the Bumbungan River, also known as the Pagsanjan River.
