Review about Laguna

This section of the website is dedicated to showcasing the thoughts and opinions of travelers who have visited the province of Laguna. These reviews provide valuable insights into their overall experience, including the sights they visited, the local culture they encountered, and the service they received from businesses and locals. By reading these reviews, potential visitors can get a better idea of what to expect when planning their own trip to Laguna.

If you enjoy and love nature, you'll probably then I could say that Majayjay Falls is the place for you. You'll not only enjoy the falls but also the surrounding of it, there are plenty of trees. Upon entering the vicinity, it will take 10 minutes to reach the falls. Aside from this, Majayjah Falls has a hanging bridge where you can take a picture. If I were gonna talk about the entrance fee, I could say that it's very cheap 'coz the entrance fee cost 50 pesos per head, if I'm not mistaken.
